The documentary approach in filmmaking is to capture life as it honestly is. What makes your life unique among all the others lies in the details.

The process on your wedding day is to preserve those intricate details. The inbetween moments, the atmosphere, the way you look at one another on your wedding day and realize it's actually happening. These provide the authentic capture of your love.

The story of your love, as it stands, right now- that you will be able to relive throughout the changing of time.
